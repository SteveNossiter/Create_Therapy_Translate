Information & Security

Quick Start Instructions

  1. Prepare: Ensure client consent is granted and documented.
  2. Record: Tap the Record stone. The screen will lock in an "awake" state to prevent the device from sleeping during the session.
  3. Process: Tap Stop, then Generate. Transcription usually takes 10–30 seconds depending on session length.
  4. Export: Copy or download the notes into your primary patient management system.
  5. Secure: Close this browser tab when finished to instantly clear all session data.

Security & Compliance

  • Data Sovereignty: Authentication is routed through onshore servers (Oceania/Sydney), ensuring credentials do not leave Australian jurisdiction.
  • Ephemeral Processing: Session audio is transmitted via secure, encrypted channels (TLS 1.2+) strictly for processing. The audio is immediately discarded by the AI server upon transcription and is never used to train public machine learning models.
  • Zero-Footprint Storage: This application operates ephemerally. Login tokens are destroyed the moment this browser tab is closed. Temporary local audio backups (to prevent data loss during internet outages) are encrypted by the browser and automatically hard-deleted.
  • Clinical Consent: As per standard clinical governance, the therapist assumes responsibility for obtaining explicit, informed consent from the client prior to initiating the recording.
