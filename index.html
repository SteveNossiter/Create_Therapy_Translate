Information & Security

Quick Start Instructions

  1. Prepare: Enter the client's initials and select your desired report template.
  2. Record: Tap the Record stone. Your device screen will automatically lock awake.
  3. Process: Tap Stop, then Generate. The audio uploads instantly.
  4. Clear Workspace: You can safely click 'Clear Session' immediately. The cloud processes the audio in the background and notifies you when the notes land in your Archive.

Global & Regional Standards

  • Australia (Privacy Act 1988 & APPs): This application enforces strict data minimization. Audio is processed transiently and never stored.
  • United States (HIPAA): Enterprise-grade API endpoints guarantee that Protected Health Information (PHI) is isolated. Data is explicitly prohibited from being used to train Google’s models.

Security Architecture Update

  • Asynchronous Server-Side Encryption (SSE-C): To allow you to safely close your browser tab during processing, your notes are encrypted locally in the memory (RAM) of our onshore servers using your Vault Key before hitting the database. The server immediately destroys the key.
  • Row Level Security (RLS): The database physically blocks access to any records that do not belong to your specific authenticated user token.

Client Consent Template

Copy and paste this clause into your intake paperwork or practice privacy policy to ensure informed consent:

I consent to the use of an AI-assisted secure transcription tool during my sessions to assist my therapist in clinical note-taking. I understand that the audio is transmitted via encrypted channels strictly for transient processing and is immediately destroyed. Audio is never stored and is explicitly prohibited from being used to train public AI models. All generated clinical notes are secured using zero-knowledge encryption in compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), HIPAA, and GDPR standards.
