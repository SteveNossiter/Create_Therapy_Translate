Security & Compliance

Global & Regional Standards

  • Australia (Privacy Act 1988 & APPs): This application enforces strict data minimization. Audio is processed transiently and never stored. Clinical notes are encrypted client-side, ensuring compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles.
  • United States (HIPAA): Enterprise-grade API endpoints guarantee that Protected Health Information (PHI) is isolated. Data is explicitly prohibited from being used to train Google’s public foundation models.
  • Europe (GDPR): You retain full data sovereignty. The "Soft Delete" function satisfies clinical retention laws, while maintaining compliance with processing limitation requirements.

Security Architecture

  • Zero-Knowledge Encryption: Notes are encrypted locally in your browser using AES-GCM via the Web Crypto API before they ever hit the database. Only your password can unlock them.
  • Row Level Security (RLS): The database physically blocks access to any records that do not belong to your specific authenticated user token, protecting against cross-tenant breaches.

Client Consent Template

Copy and paste this clause into your intake paperwork or practice privacy policy to ensure informed consent:

I consent to the use of an AI-assisted secure transcription tool during my sessions to assist my therapist in clinical note-taking. I understand that the audio is transmitted via encrypted channels strictly for transient processing and is immediately destroyed. Audio is never stored and is explicitly prohibited from being used to train public AI models. All generated clinical notes are secured using zero-knowledge encryption in compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), HIPAA, and GDPR standards.
